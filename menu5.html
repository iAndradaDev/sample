Pork Adobo

₱169.00 - Large Meal (bigger serving with rice + drink)

Pork Adobo is a classic Filipino dish made from tender pork simmered slowly in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns. The meat becomes rich, savory, and slightly tangy, absorbing the deep flavors of the sauce. It is best served with hot steamed rice and sometimes topped with a bit of sauce for extra flavor.
