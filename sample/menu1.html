Chicken Inasal

₱149.00 - Regular Meal (1 piece with rice + drink)

Chicken Inasal is a traditional grilled chicken dish from Bacolod, marinated in calamansi, vinegar, garlic, pepper, and annatto oil. It is charcoal-grilled to achieve a smoky aroma and juicy, flavorful meat. It is typically served with rice, soy sauce, vinegar dip, and atchara.
