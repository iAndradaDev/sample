Pork BBQ

₱129.00 - 3 sticks with rice & drink

Pork BBQ is a popular Filipino street food made from tender pork slices marinated in a sweet and savory blend of soy sauce, calamansi, garlic, sugar, and spices. The meat is skewered and grilled over hot charcoal, giving it a smoky flavor with a slightly caramelized glaze. It is usually served with rice and a special BBQ sauce or vinegar dip.
