Pork Belly

₱219 - Large Meal (extra serving with rice + drink)

Pork Belly is a rich and flavorful dish made from thick slices of pork belly that are seasoned and grilled or fried until crispy on the outside while staying juicy and tender inside. It has a savory, slightly smoky taste and is best enjoyed with steamed rice and a dipping sauce like soy sauce with calamansi or spiced vinegar.
